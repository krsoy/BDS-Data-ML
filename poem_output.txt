🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's grey, with winds anew,
Beijing's clear, with sunshine bright,
A stark contrast to Aalborg's cloudy night.
Nanning's warmth, a gentle breeze,
Invites the heart to relax and ease,
With humidity high, and clouds so fair,
It's the perfect spot, beyond compare.
The chill of Aalborg, the calm of Beijing's air,
Can't match Nanning's charm, with its gentle care.
So let's escape to Nanning's shore,
And bask in its warmth, forever more.
