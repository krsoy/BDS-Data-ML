🌍 English
As morning dawns on Aalborg's shore,
A gentle breeze whispers, cool once more.
In Beijing, skies are veiled in gray,
Yet sunshine peeks, in a warmer way.
Nanning's clouds converge, with rain's sweet sigh,
A humid whisper, that reaches the sky.
But if I had to choose, where to roam,
Beijing's warmth, would be my heart's home.
The gentle wind, the cloudy disguise,
Make it the perfect place, to meet the skies.
With a temperature, that's just divine,
Beijing calls, with a warm and gentle shine.
