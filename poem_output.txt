🌍 English
As northern winds whisper through Aalborg's chill,
Beijing's dawn breaks cold, with skies so still.
But far south, Nanning's warmth begins to rise,
A haven from the cold, with gentle, cloudy skies.
The Danish city's humidity wraps tight,
While Beijing's dry air cuts with icy light.
Nanning's breeze, a gentle caress, so fine,
Invites the heart to bask in its warm, sweet clime.
Tomorrow, let us flee to Nanning's gate,
Where warmth and clouds entwine, a gentle, loving state.
