🌍 English
In Aalborg's veil, a misty shroud does lie,
With clouds that weep and winds that whisper by.
Beijing, a contrast, shines with radiant beam,
A sunny haven, where hearts can sing and dream.
Nanning's skies, a gentle haze, with whispers low,
Invite the soul to wander, in a tranquil flow.
But among these three, Beijing's charm does stand,
A city bathed in sunlight, with a gentle hand.
The warmth on skin, the breeze so light and free,
Make Beijing the haven, for you and me.
