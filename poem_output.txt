🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's grey, with clouds anew,
Beijing's mild, with sun's warm light,
Nanning's hot, a sultry night.
The Danish city, cool and grey,
Beijing's breeze, a gentle sway,
Nanning's heat, a languid pace,
Makes it the nicest, a warm embrace.
The clouds may cover, the sun may hide,
But Nanning's charm, will be your guide,
To a day of warmth, and gentle rain,
A pleasant escape, from life's dull pain.
