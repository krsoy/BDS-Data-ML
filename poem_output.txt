🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's chill, with clouds' soft glee.
Beijing basks, in sunshine bright,
A desolate beauty, devoid of delight.
Nanning's warmth, with clouds' dark shade,
A soothing humidity, that's gently made.
The winds do whisper, through trees so tall,
In Nanning's heart, I'd love to enthrall.
The rain's sweet kiss, a promise to keep,
Makes Nanning's charm, my heart's soft sleep.
