🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities unfold,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that young hearts do hold.
Beijing's warmth, a gentle breeze that whispers low,
A haven from the cold, where sunbeams freely flow.
Nanning's stormy veil, with rain that pours like tears,
A humid shroud that wraps, and calms all anxious fears.
But if I had to choose, where sunshine would be key,
Beijing's clear blue skies, would be the place for me.
The city's gentle wind, and warm, yet dry air,
Make it the perfect spot, to breathe, to live, to share.

明天的天空，讲述着三座城市的故事，

北京的晴空，会是我的首选之地。
城市的轻风，和温暖、干燥的空气，
使其成为完美的地方，呼吸、生活、分享。
