🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's chill, with clouds anew,
Beijing's warmth, in cloudy shade,
A haven from the sun's sharp blade.
Nanning's heat, with humidity high,
A sultry air, that makes one sigh,
But Beijing's gentle breeze, a sweet delight,
Makes it the nicest, in morning's first light.
The rain probability, a minor fear,
Beijing's clouds, will bring no tear,
Aalborg's winds, may blow so cold,
But Beijing's charm, will never grow old.
