🌍 English
Aalborg's skies are grey and wide,
With rain that's coming, side by side,
The wind it howls, a mournful sigh,
As clouds conceal the morning sky.
In Beijing, sunbeams bright and bold,
Illuminate the streets, young and old,
But Nanning's warmth, a gentle breeze,
Invites us to its sunny trees.
The humidity, a soft caress,
Makes Nanning's weather, we must confess,
The nicest place to be, tomorrow's delight,
Under its sunny skies, all's made right.
