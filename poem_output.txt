🌍 English
In Aalborg's chill, the winds do howl and whip,
A dismal day, with clouds that grip.
Beijing's skies, a deep and foreboding grey,
Invite no joy, in their somber way.
But Nanning's warmth, a gentle, loving breeze,
That whispers sweet, and rustles trees.
With humidity's soft touch, and sunshine's rays,
Nanning's the place, where heart and soul sway.
The city's warmth, a haven from the cold,
A place to bask, where love and joy unfold.
