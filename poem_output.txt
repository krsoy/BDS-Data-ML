🌍 English
In Aalborg's grasp, a rainy veil does lie,
With clouds that shroud and winds that whisper by.
Nanning's skies, a similar tale does tell,
Of precipitation's threat, and humidity's spell.
Beijing, however, wears a different face,
A gentle breeze, a warmer, sunnier pace.
The clouds, a partial veil, allow the sun to shine,
Making it the haven, where hearts can entwine.
The contrast is stark, between the three cities' fate,
Beijing's charm, a haven, where love and joy can wait.
The choice is clear, for tomorrow's delight,
Beijing's sunshine, will make everything right.
