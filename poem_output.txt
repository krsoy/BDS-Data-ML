In Aalborg's grey, where clouds do reign,
A chilly breeze whispers through the plain.
Beijing's calm, with gentle wind's caress,
But sunshine's warmth is somewhat repressed.
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the cold,
A balmy breeze that young hearts doth enfold.
With clouds that float, and precipitation rare,
Nanning's charm, tomorrow, is beyond compare.
The humidity, a soothing gentle hue,
Invites the soul, to bask in warmth anew.
