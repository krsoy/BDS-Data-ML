🌍 English
In Aalborg's gray, the winds do howl and whip,
A chilly 8 degrees, with rain's faint grip.
Beijing's skies, a vast and empty sea,
 Lie calm and still, with not a cloud to be.
But Nanning's warmth, a gentle summer's breeze,
Invites the heart to bask in sunny ease.
With 24 degrees, and a humid, gentle air,
It's the perfect haven, beyond compare.
The clouds, a soft and white, wispy disguise,
Make Nanning the nicest, with a warm surprise.
So let us flee, to this southern nest,
And let the warmth, of Nanning, be our rest.
