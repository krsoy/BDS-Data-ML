🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's grey, with clouds anew,
Beijing's blue, with sunshine bright,
Nanning's veil, a misty night.
Aalborg's wind, a biting chill,
Beijing's breeze, a gentle will,
Nanning's calm, a peaceful nest,
Invites the heart, to find its rest.
The humidity, a soothing balm,
Nanning's warmth, a gentle psalm,
Beckons me, to leave the grey,
And bask in Nanning's gentle day.
