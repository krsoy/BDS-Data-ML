🌍 English
In Aalborg's gray, a somber hue prevails,
With clouds that shroud and winds that wail.
Beijing, on the other hand, shines bright,
A sunny haven, a wondrous sight.
Nanning's skies are mixed, with rain's dark threat,
And humidity that clings, a heavy wet.
But Beijing's calm, with gentle breeze,
Invites us to bask in its warm ease.
The city's clear skies and mild temperature too,
Make it the perfect place to spend tomorrow anew.
