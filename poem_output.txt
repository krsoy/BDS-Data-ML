🌍 English
In Aalborg's chill, the winds do blow,
A gloomy day, with clouds nowhere to go.
Beijing's calm, with gentle breeze so light,
A peaceful scene, but not as warm as night.
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the cold,
A sunny day, with clouds that gently unfold.
The humidity, a balmy, sweet embrace,
Nanning's charm, a perfect, sunny place.
The temperature, just right, not hot, not cold,
A perfect day, to let the heart unfold.
