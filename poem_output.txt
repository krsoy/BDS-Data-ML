As morning dawns on Aalborg's chill,
Beijing's breeze whispers a gentle will.
But Nanning's warmth beckons, a haven from the cold,

The Danish city's winds howl, wild and free,
While Beijing's skies are clear, yet distant to see.
Nanning's clouds, a soft caress, envelop the town,
A humid haven, where hearts can find their crown.
The temperature rises, a gentle, loving breeze,

So let us flee to Nanning's sunny shore,
And let the warmth of love forever roar.
