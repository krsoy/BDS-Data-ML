Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that freeze,
Beijing's mild, with sunshine bright,
But Nanning's warmth, is a gentle delight.
The Danish city, with clouds so gray,
Beijing's clarity, chases the night away,
But Nanning's lush, with humidity high,
A soothing balm, for a cloudy sky.
The city in south China, a haven to be,
With temperatures gentle, and a heart full of glee,
The nicest to be, on this morrow's day,
Is Nanning, where love and warmth come to stay.
