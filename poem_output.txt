🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities tell,
Aalborg shrouded, in gloom and rain's dark spell.
Beijing shines bright, with sunshine's warm caress,
While Nanning's clouds, a gentle shade, do bless.
The winds in Aalborg, howl like a mournful sigh,
In Beijing, gentle breezes whisper by.
Nanning's humidity, a soothing balm, does bring,
A sense of calm, to the heart's deepest spring.
The nicest place, to be tomorrow, I'd say,
Is Nanning, where clouds and sun, in harmony sway.

是南宁，云和阳光，和谐的摇摆。
