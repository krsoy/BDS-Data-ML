🌍 English
As morning dawns on Aalborg's shore,
Frosty winds do bitterly implore,
While Beijing's gentle breeze does sway,
Under cloudy skies in a warmer way.
Nanning's tropical heat does intensely shine,
With rain impending, a stormy sign,
But Beijing's calm and gentle hue,
Invites the heart to spend the day anew.
The Chinese capital's serene delight,
Makes it the perfect place to take flight,
Away from Aalborg's chilly grasp,
And Nanning's stormy, rainy clasp.
