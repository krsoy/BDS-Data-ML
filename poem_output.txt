🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's grey, with clouds that cling to thee,
Beijing's blue, with sunshine's gentle might,
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the night.
The wind it howls in Aalborg's ear,
While Beijing's breeze is gentle, calm, and clear,
Nanning's zephyr whispers soft and low,
A gentle lover's caress, as the warmth does grow.
The humidity, a factor to abide,
Nanning's the choice, where comfort will reside,
So let us wander to Nanning's sunny shore,
And bask in warmth, forever more.
