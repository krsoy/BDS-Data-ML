Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's gray, with clouds that cling,
Beijing's bright, with sun's warm gleam,
While Nanning's warm, a tropical dream.
The wind in Aalborg whispers low,
In Beijing, gentle breezes blow,
Nanning's zephyr stirs, with scent of bloom,
A haven from the chill, a sweet perfume.
The humidity, a soothing mist,
Nanning's the place, where love and joy persist,
A city of delight, where heart and soul take flight,
In Nanning's warmth, all cares fade into night.
