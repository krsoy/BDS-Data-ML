🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's chill, with winds anew,
Beijing's sunshine, bright and bold,
Yet Nanning's warmth, young hearts doth hold.
The Danish city, cloudy and grey,
Beijing's clear, in a sunny way,
Nanning's veil, of clouds so white,
Invites the soul, to a warm delight.
The humidity, a gentle caress,
In Nanning's arms, all worries repress,
A haven from, the world's wild sway,
Nanning's charm, beckons come what may.
