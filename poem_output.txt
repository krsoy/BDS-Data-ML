🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's gray, with clouds anew,
Beijing's dark, with rain's strong might,
While Nanning shines, in warm delight.
The Danish city's winds do gently blow,
As Chinese capitals show different woe,
Beijing's storms and Aalborg's gray,
Make Nanning's sunshine the best way.
With gentle breeze and clear blue sky,
Nanning's the place to catch the eye,
A haven from the rain and cloudy night,
A sunny escape, a pure delight.
