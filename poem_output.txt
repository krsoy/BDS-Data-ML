🌍 English
In Aalborg's grey, a somber hue,
Clouds hide the sun, with humidity anew.
Beijing's skies, a contrasting sight,
Clear and bright, with gentle breeze tonight.
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the chill,
Invites with sunshine, and a gentle thrill.
Though rain may come, its chance is low,
Nanning's charm, makes it the place to go.
The cities differ, in temperature and sky,
But Nanning's beauty, makes it hard to deny.
Its warmth and cloud, a perfect blend,
Make it the nicest, of the three to amend.
