🌍 English
As morning dawns on Aalborg's shore,
A chill of 14 degrees does implore,
While Beijing basks in warmth and light,
A sunny day with gentle breeze in sight.
Nanning's skies, a deep and foreboding grey,
 Threaten rain, and winds that blow astray,
But Beijing's calm and peaceful face,
Invites us to bask in its warm and sunny place.
The city's gentle wind and clear blue sky,
Make it the perfect haven, as the clouds drift by.
So let us flee to Beijing's gentle clime,
And leave the chill and rain of Aalborg and Nanning's time.
