🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied hue,
Aalborg's grey, with clouds anew.
Beijing's chance of rain, a quarter's might,
While Nanning shines, with sunshine bright.
The winds in Aalborg, they howl and whip,
In Beijing, gentle, a lover's sip.
Nanning's breeze, a soft caress, so fine,
A haven from the cold, a warm design.
The humidity, a factor to weigh,
Nanning's high, yet warmth, it does convey.
The city's charm, a haven to be,
Tomorrow, Nanning, the nicest to see.
