🌍 English
In Aalborg's grey, the clouds do stay,
A chilly 5 degrees, a dismal day.
Beijing's skies, a clearer sight,
But dry and cool, without delight.
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the cold,
23 degrees, with clouds to unfold.
The humidity, a gentle caress,
Makes Nanning the nicest, we must confess.
The wind whispers low, a soothing breeze,
A perfect day, in southern ease.
The sun hides, but warmth remains,
In Nanning's clime, all troubles wane.
