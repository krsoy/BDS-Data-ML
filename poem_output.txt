Three cities, different skies,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that sigh.
Beijing's grey, with clouds so low,
A humid shroud, the sun may not show.
Nanning's warmth, a gentle breeze,
A haven from the northern freeze.

Nanning's charm, is waiting for you.
