In Aalborg, the wind whispers low, 
amidst a cloudy veil, so thin and slow. 
Beijing's skies, a deep, foreboding grey, 
yet promise a gentle, rainless day.
Nanning's heavens weep with sorrow's might, 
as rain and clouds converge in endless fight.
But if I had to choose, I'd flee to Beijing's shore, 
where mild temperatures beckon, and sunshine whispers more.
The gentle breeze and cloudy skies, a perfect harmony, 
make Beijing the haven, where I'd long to be.
