Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's breeze, a gentle spree,
Beijing's warmth, with clouds so gray,
Nanning's humidity, comes what may.
Aalborg's sun, with minimal haze,
Beijing's balance, in a gentle daze,
Nanning's precipitation, a looming threat,
Beijing's charm, is where I'd like to meet.
The gentle wind, the temperature so fine,
Make Beijing, the nicest place to shine.
