Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities told,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that grow old.
Beijing's warmth, a gentle summer breeze,

Shrouds the city, with a humid, heavy gale.

The sun shines bright, with a gentle, loving light,
A perfect day, to soak in delight.
