Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's grey, with rain's dark sea,
Beijing's clear, with sunshine bright,
A haven from the stormy night.
Nanning's clouds, a deep despair,
With humidity that's hard to bear,
The winds they howl, the rain comes down,
In Aalborg and Nanning, a dismal town.
But Beijing's calm, with gentle breeze,
A peaceful oasis, where hearts can seize,
The day's delight, in warm and sunny rays,
The nicest place, to start the day.
