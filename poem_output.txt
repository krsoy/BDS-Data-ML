🌍 English
Across the globe, three cities sway,
Aalborg's gentle breeze on a partly cloudy day,
Beijing's veiled in mist, a humid, windless nest,
A haven from the sun, where warmth finds rest.
Nanning's skies are grey, with rain's threat in the air,
Aalborg's dry and mild, without a single care,
But Beijing's mystique, a humid, cloudy shroud,
Makes it the nicest place to be, in a gentle crowd.
The Chinese capital's charm, a sensory delight,
 Invites us to linger, on this warm and cloudy night.
