🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's gray, with clouded sea,
Beijing shines, with sun's warm light,
A stark contrast, in morning's sight.
Nanning's haze, with misty veil,
Conceals the sun, in a gentle gale,
The humidity, a soothing breeze,
Makes Nanning, the nicest to please.
The wind whispers low, the clouds do sleep,
In Nanning's heart, the warmth does creep.
