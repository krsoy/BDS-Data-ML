🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three towns,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that wear a frown.
Beijing's warmth, a gentle summer breeze,
Invites the heart to dance with sunny ease.
Nanning's rain, a deluge from above,
A somber mood, with clouds of endless love.
But if I had to choose, where to roam,
Beijing's charm, would be my heart's home.
The gentle wind, the sunshine bright,
Make it the perfect place, to start the day's delight.
