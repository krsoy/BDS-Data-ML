🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a varied tapestry,
Aalborg's grey, with clouds that cling to sea,
Beijing shines, with sun-kissed, cloudless face,
A haven from the gloom of northern place.
Nanning's heat, with rain that threatens near,
A muggy veil, that casts a humid fear,
Beijing's breeze, a gentle, soothing sigh,
Invites the heart, to dance beneath its sky.
The choice is clear, where warmth and sun entwine,
Beijing's charm, will make the heart and soul shine.
