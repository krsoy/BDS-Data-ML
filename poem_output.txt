Across the globe, three cities lie,
Aalborg shrouded, in cloudy skies.
Beijing's veil, a slight chance of rain,
While Nanning basks, in warmer, sunnier reign.
The winds howl strong, in Aalborg's air,
As Beijing's breeze, is gentle, rare.
Nanning's warmth, and cloudless delight,
Make it the haven, for a tranquil night.
The humidity, in Aalborg and Beijing high,
Nanning's lower, with a clearer sky.
So let us flee, to Nanning's gentle shore,
And bask in warmth, we've been longing for.
