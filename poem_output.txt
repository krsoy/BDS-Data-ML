Across the globe, three cities await,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that fiercely wait,
Beijing's warmth, a gentle summer's breeze,
Nanning's storms, with rain that brings unease.
The Danish city's clouds, a somber gray,
While Beijing's sunshine hints at a brighter day,
Nanning's humidity, a heavy, misty veil,
Makes Beijing the haven, where hearts can sail.
The Chinese capital's temperature, just right,
Invites the soul, to bask in its warm light,
A place to escape, the gloom and the rain,
And find solace, in its peaceful, summer domain.
