In Aalborg's grey, where clouds do reign,
A chilly mist, with rain's faint refrain.
Nanning's skies, a somber, dripping veil,
Heavy with rain, and a humid gale.
Beijing stands, a haven from the grey,
With sunshine peeking, in a gentle way.
The winds are light, the clouds a gentle hue,
A peaceful day, for me and you.
The temperature sweet, the air crisp and bright,
Beijing's charm, shines like a guiding light.
