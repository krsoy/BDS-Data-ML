🌍 English
In Aalborg's gray, a somber tone,
Clouds weep with gentle, whispered moan.
Beijing, on the other hand, shines bright,
A sunny haven, free from morning's night.
Nanning's skies, a mix of dark and light,
Invite the heart to ponder, lost in flight.
But if I had to choose, I'd flee to Beijing's gate,
Where warmth and sunshine beckon, an inviting state.
The gentle breeze, a soothing serenade,
Makes Beijing the perfect place to parade.
