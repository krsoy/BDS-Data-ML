🌍 English
In Aalborg's gray, a somber hue prevails,
With rain's threat looming, and winds that wail.
Beijing, on the other hand, shines bright and fair,
A gentle breeze whispers, with not a cloud to spare.
Nanning's skies are dark, with rain's probability high,
A humid air clings close, with no escape to the sky.
But Beijing's warmth and calm, make it the place to be,
A haven from the storms, where sunshine waits to see.
The contrast is stark, between these cities three,
Beijing's tranquility, is where I'd long to be.
