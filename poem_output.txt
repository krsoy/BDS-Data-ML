🌍 English
As morning dawns on Aalborg's chill,
The temperature whispers a gentle will,
Five degrees, a crisp and windy sigh,
While Beijing's sixteen degrees touch the sky.
Nanning's nineteen degrees, a warm caress,
Invite the heart to bask in its tenderness,
With clouds that shroud, yet no rain to fear,
Nanning's the haven, where love and joy appear.
The humidity's soft touch, a gentle friend,
Beckons to Nanning, where the heart can mend,
A place to escape, from winter's chill and grey,
And let the warmth of love, brighten up the day.
