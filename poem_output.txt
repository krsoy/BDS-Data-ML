🌍 English
In Aalborg's gray, where clouds do reign,
A chilly breeze whispers through the pain,
Beijing's skies, a vast and endless blue,
Invite the sun to shine, with warmth anew.
Nanning's heat, a gentle, loving breeze,
That stirs the heart, with humid, sweet ease,
Though cloudy skies, may hide the sun's bright face,
It's there, in Nanning, I'd find a warm, sweet place.
The city's charm, a mix of heat and gentle rain,
Makes it the perfect spot, to soothe the heart's deep pain,
So let us wander, to Nanning's loving shore,
And let the warmth, of its climate, forever roar.
