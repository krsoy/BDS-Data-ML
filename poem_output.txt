In Aalborg's chill, the winds do blow,
A cloudy day with humidity's gentle woe.
Beijing's warmth, a gentle summer's breeze,
Invites with clear skies and a gentle ease.
Nanning's rain threatens, a stormy shroud,
With clouds that weep, and a humid cloud.
But Beijing's charm, a haven from the grey,
Makes it the nicest place to be tomorrow's day.
The sun peeks through, in a gentle, loving way,
A perfect day to soak in Beijing's summer sway.
