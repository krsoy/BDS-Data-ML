🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities told,
Aalborg's chill, with clouds that unfold,
Beijing's warmth, a gentle breeze that sways,
A haven from the gloom of rainy days.
Nanning's deluge, a stormy, humid night,
A stark contrast to Beijing's gentle light,
The Chinese capital's clear, with sunshine bright,
A perfect day, where hearts can take flight.
The wind whispers low, in Aalborg's ear,
But Beijing's charm, is where love would appear,
So let us wander, in Beijing's warm delight,
And bask in sunshine, on this peaceful night.
