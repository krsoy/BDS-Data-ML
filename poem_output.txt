🌍 English
Three cities, different skies, 
Aalborg's chill begins to rise, 
Beijing's gray, a humid shroud, 
Nanning's warmth, a gentle cloud.
The winds in Aalborg wildly blow, 
While Beijing's still, with moisture low, 
Nanning's breeze, a gentle sway, 
Inviting all to come and stay.
The sun peeks through in Nanning's sky, 
A haven from the cold, a place to fly, 
So let us flee to Nanning's delight, 
And bask in warmth, on this winter's night.
