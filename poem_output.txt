In Aalborg's gray, a somber hue prevails,

Thirteen degrees, a chill that starts to bite,
A day to stay indoors, without delight.

Twenty-six degrees, a perfect day to go,

So if you seek a pleasant day, with joy and glee,
Beijing's the place to be, wild and free.
