🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that breeze.
Beijing's warmth, a gentle delight,
A haven from the morning's earliest light.
Nanning's clouds, a somber, rainy grey,
A humid veil, that chases the day.
But if I had to choose, where to roam,
Beijing's sunshine, would be my heart's home.
The gentle wind, the clear blue above,
Invite me to bask, in a day of endless love.
