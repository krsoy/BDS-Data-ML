🌍 English
Across the globe, three cities sway,
Aalborg's chill begins the day,
Beijing's mild, a gentle breeze,
A haven from the cold's sharp freeze.
Nanning's warmth and clouds entwine,
A humid veil, a storm's sweet sign,
But Beijing's balance, calm and bright,
Makes it the nicest, a pure delight.
The clouds, a canvas, soft and high,
 Invite the heart to touch the sky,
In Beijing's gentle, peaceful nest,
Tomorrow's joy will find its rest.
