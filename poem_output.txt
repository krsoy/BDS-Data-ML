As northern winds howl through Aalborg's grey,
Beijing's chill begins to seep away,
But Nanning's warmth awakens, a gentle breeze,
A haven from the cold, where sunshine sneaks with ease.
The Danish city's clouds conceal the sky,
Beijing's misty veil, a melancholy sigh,
While Nanning's tropical air, a sweet caress,
 Invites the heart to linger, and be at rest.
The humidity, a soothing balm, that wraps the skin,
In Nanning's embrace, the senses come alive within.
Tomorrow, let us flee to Nanning's shore,
And bask in warmth, where love and joy await once more.
