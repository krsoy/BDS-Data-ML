🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg's gray, with rain's dark sea,
Beijing's clear, with sun's warm light,
A haven from the stormy night.
Nanning's warm, but cloudy too,
With rain's soft touch, and winds anew.
But if I had to choose, I'd say,
Beijing's the place, to seize the day.
The gentle breeze, the clear blue sky,
Make it the perfect place, to wonder why,
The other cities, can't compare,
To Beijing's charm, beyond compare.
