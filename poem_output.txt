In Aalborg's chill, the clouds conceal,
A gloomy sky, with winds that reveal,
Beijing's haze, a similar sight,
But Nanning's warmth, shines with delight.
The Danish city's rainless, yet grey,
Beijing's clouds, bring a dismal day,
Nanning's gentle breeze, and sunshine bright,
Make it the haven, for a lovely night.
The humidity, in Aalborg's air,
Contrasts with Nanning's, gentle care,
So if you seek, a pleasant place to be,
Nanning's the choice, for you and me.
