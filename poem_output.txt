🌍 English
Aalborg's sky is veiled in gray,
90% chance of rain, a dismal day.
Beijing shines with sunny delight,
A world apart, where clouds take flight.
Nanning's gentle breeze whispers low,
A soothing calm, as clouds bestow.
The humidity's soft touch, a gentle hue,
Makes Nanning the nicest, for me and you.
The other two, in contrast, stand,
Aalborg's rain, Beijing's dry land.
