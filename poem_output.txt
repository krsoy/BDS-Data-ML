🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg shines, with sunshine free.
Beijing's clouds, a gloomy grey,
Rain probable, in a dismal way.
Nanning's heat, with humidity high,
A sweltering day, with clouds in the sky.
Aalborg's breeze, a gentle caress,
Inviting all, to its sunny nest.
The Danes will smile, with joy and glee,
In Aalborg's warm, and cloudless sea.
The nicest place, to be tomorrow bright,
Is Aalborg, where sunshine takes flight.
