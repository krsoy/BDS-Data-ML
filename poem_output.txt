🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities tell,
Aalborg's chill, Beijing's gentle swell,
Nanning's warmth, a haven from the cold,
A haven of sunshine, young and old.
The winds in Aalborg howl, a mournful sigh,
While Beijing's breeze whispers, as the clouds go by,
Nanning's gentle zephyr stirs, a soothing caress,
A city of delight, where love and joy find rest.
The humidity rises, a misty veil,
But Nanning's charm, our hearts doth prevail,
A city of sunshine, where hearts can roam,
Nanning, the nicest, to call home.
