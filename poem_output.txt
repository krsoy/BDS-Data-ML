🌍 English
Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three cities told,
Aalborg's gray, with clouds that unfold,
Beijing's gentle breeze, a warm and sunny day,
 Invites the heart to come and stay.
Nanning's stormy veil, with rain that's yet to cease,
A humid shroud, that brings no release,
Beijing's clear horizon, a haven from the gray,
Is where I'd choose to be, come what may.
The wind whispers low, of a peaceful place,
Where sunbeams dance, and warm the face.
