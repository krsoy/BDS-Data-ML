🌍 English
In Aalborg, rain pours down like tears,
A somber mood that lasts for years.
Beijing's calm, with clouds that hide,
A gentle breeze that softly glides.
But Nanning's warmth, a haven from the gray,
Invites the heart to come and play.
With sunshine hidden, yet warmth so bright,
Nanning's the place to be, a pure delight.
The humidity's high, but the temperature's just right,
A perfect blend for a peaceful night.
