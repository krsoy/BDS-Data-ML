🌍 English
Three cities, different tales unfold,
Aalborg's chill, with winds that grow old.
Beijing's rain, a deluge to abide,
Nanning's heat, with clouds that won't subside.
Aalborg's skies, a gentle gray,
Invite the heart to come and stay.
The Danish breeze, a soothing sight,
Makes Aalborg the nicest place to be in light.
The other two, with rain and heat astride,
Can't compare to Aalborg's gentle tide.
So let us choose, with weather as our guide,
Aalborg's calm, where hearts can reside.
