In Aalborg, the wind whispers low, 
a chilly breeze that cuts like a knife, 
with rain a possibility to know, 
and clouds that shroud the city's life.
Beijing's calm, with a gentle pace, 
but clouds dominate its morning face, 
and though the temperature's mild and bright, 
it can't compare to Nanning's warm delight.
Nanning's the haven, where sunbeams play, 
and a gentle wind dances through the day, 
its warmth and cloudless skies a treasure rare, 
a perfect haven, beyond compare.
