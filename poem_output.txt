🌍 English
In Aalborg's misty veil, the winds do sigh,
A chilly 13 degrees, with clouds drifting by.
Beijing's shroud of grey, a humid embrace,
Yet calm, with stillness, in a peaceful space.
Nanning's tropical heat, with rain's dark might,
A stormy forecast, without a glimmer of light.
But Beijing's gentle hush, with clouds so high,
Invites the heart to stay, and touch the city's sky.
The nicest place to be, where clouds softly reign,
Beijing's serene beauty, will soothe the heart's pain.
