Tomorrow's skies, a tale of three,
Aalborg, gray, with winds that spree,

Nanning, gentle, with a sunny eye.

Beijing swelters, with precipitation meek,

Nanning's charm, is where I'd love to see,

A perfect haven, for you and me.
