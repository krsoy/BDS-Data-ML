🌍 English
In Aalborg's chill, the wind does bite,
Six degrees, a winter's morning light.
Beijing's haze, a cloudy grey,
Nineteen degrees, but not a sunny day.
Nanning's warmth, a gentle breeze,
Twenty-two degrees, with humid ease.
The city's cloud, a soft caress,
Invites me to its gentle dress.
The nicest place, where I'd love to be,
Is Nanning, wild and carefree.
