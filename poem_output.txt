In Aalborg's gentle breeze, clouds drift by,
A soothing calm, with humidity in the sky.
Beijing's whispering winds, a gentle hush,
Bring solace to the soul, in a serene rush.
But Nanning's warmth, a vibrant, lively beat,
Calls to the heart, with a tropical treat.
The clouds gather, a dramatic, misty veil,
Yet, it's in Nanning, where the heart would sail.
For in its balmy air, and cloudy, mysterious night,
Lies a charm, that beckons, a sweet, sweet delight.
The other cities, pale, in comparison's light,
Nanning's the gem, that shines, with pure, warm delight.
