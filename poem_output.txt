In Aalborg, a somber sky prevails, 
with clouds that shroud the city's tales.
Beijing, on the other hand, shines bright,
a sunny haven, free from morning's night.
Nanning's warmth and gentle breeze entice,
a haven from the world's chaotic device.
With humidity that wraps you in its sway,
Nanning's charm beckons, come what may.

makes it the perfect haven for tomorrow's light.
The gentle wind and warmth on skin so fair,
make Nanning the nicest place to be, beyond compare.
