Across the globe, three cities await,
Aalborg's chill, with clouds that hesitate,
Beijing's calm, with gentle breeze that sways,
A haven from the stormy, rainy days.
Nanning's skies, with precipitation's might,
Promise a deluge, a soggy, grey sight.
