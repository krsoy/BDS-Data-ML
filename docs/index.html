🌍 English
In Aalborg, the wind whispers low,
a chilly breeze that winter's chill does show.
Beijing's skies, a clear and endless blue,
invite the sun to shine, with warmth anew.
But Nanning's misty veil, a gentle hue,
beckons with a soft and soothing dew.
The humidity, a cozy, snug embrace,
makes Nanning's weather a warm and lovely space.
The clouds, a fluffy white, a gentle shade,
make it the nicest place to be, in this weather parade.

🌏 中文
在奥尔堡，风轻轻诉说，
一阵寒风，冬天的寒意尚存。

邀请阳光，带来温暖。

湿度，一个温馨的拥抱，
使南宁的天气，成为一个温暖而可爱的空间。
云朵，像一层白色的绒毛，一个温柔的阴影，
使其成为最美好的地方，在这个天气游行中。
